AbbVie grant requests originating from the United States and Puerto Rico are managed by a centralized grant department and more information is available at grants.abbvie.com.

AbbVie discloses donations and grants made by the company because we are committed to sharing our commitment to promote health education and charitable causes that ultimately benefit patients.

The list of AbbVie’s grants and contributions are provided in the annual report below. AbbVie’s report reflects educational grants, patient support, charitable donations, as well as fellowships and scholarships. The report includes individual direct payments of more than $200 from AbbVie’s US and Puerto Rico businesses.
