Make learning and development a part of the culture

We help further education around current, new and emerging sciences and standards of care, as we know they are key to the patient experience.

Increasing treatment knowledge and disease awareness

Our grants:

  • Support telemedicine and telehealth education to foster effective patient engagement and care;
  • Help bridge the gap between academia and community and rural settings;
  • Support independent continuing education for healthcare providers with the most up-to-date information on current, new and emerging therapies;
  • Meet the needs of patients, through grants to patient organizations;
  • Support fellowships that allow healthcare providers to continue their training and increase their capacity in any given field of study; and
  • Demonstrate AbbVie’s commitment to educational resources that is independent from support of our medicines and treatments.

Therapeutic areas of focus

Our therapeutic areas of focus include immunology, oncology, neuroscience, aesthetics, eye care and other areas of scientific research with the greatest potential impact for patients.

Immunology

As a leader in the field of immunology, AbbVie is committed to supporting innovation that will continue to advance education and patient care for complex immunologic conditions.

We provide educational support in the following areas:

  • Addressing the burden of disease;Understanding new discoveries and new therapies in development; and
  • Supporting capacity-building initiatives aimed at increasing the number of healthcare providers across immunology specialties.

We will consider requests in the following areas:

Rheumatology

  • Ankylosing Spondylitis
  • Psoriatic Arthritis
  • Rheumatoid Arthritis
  • Spondyloarthritis
  • Systemic Lupus Erythematosus

Dermatology

  • Atopic Dermatitis
  • Psoriasis
  • Psoriatic Arthritis
  • Alopecia Areata
  • Vitiligo

Gastroenterology

  • Inflammatory Bowel Disease

Oncology

Combining our deep knowledge of biology with innovative technologies, AbbVie is committed to supporting education of scientific advances for those living with and treating widespread and debilitating cancers.

Oncology is a rapidly changing area. It is important to provide resources to help patients and healthcare providers navigate the evolving landscape of blood cancers and solid tumors.

We provide educational support in the following areas:

  • Staying abreast of the rapidly evolving cancer landscape and science;
  • Understanding the importance and relevance of biomarkers and cytogenetic testing;
  • Developing and enhancing skills aimed at a meaningful co-management of disease; and
  • Improving communication between patients and care team members

We will consider requests in the following areas:

Hematologic Malignancies

  • Acute Myeloid Leukemia
  • Chronic Lymphocytic Leukemia
  • Multiple Myeloma
  • Blastic Plasmacytoid Dendritic Cell Neoplasm

Solid Tumors

  • Non-Small Cell Lung Cancer
  • Ovarian Cancer

Neuroscience

Our goal is to provide education and support aimed at patients and healthcare providers managing difficult-to-treat neurological diseases as well as urologic syndromes and dysfunction associated with these conditions.

We provide educational support in the following areas:

  • Addressing the shortage of healthcare providers dedicated to the field of movement disorders; Understanding the staging of Parkinson’s Disease and corresponding appropriate therapies;
  • Educating patients and their care partners to be active and engaged participants in their care; and
  • New discoveries of neuroscience and new therapies in development.

We will consider requests in the following areas:

  • Migraine

    • Chronic
    • Episodic
    • Preventative

  • Psychiatry

    • Bipolar Depression
    • Major Depressive Disorder

  • Parkinson’s Disease

  • Spasticity/Movement Disorders

Aesthetics

With the increase in technological advances and the growing field of aesthetic medicine, we are committed to educating the healthcare community and their patients by supporting scientific, evidence-based initiatives.

We provide educational support in the following areas:

  • Supporting education on non-surgical and non-invasive techniques to achieve the best patient outcomes;
  • Enhancing clinician understanding of how to communicate, treat and manage patients; and
  • Supporting educational initiatives with the goal of achieving optimal outcomes for patients in breast implant-based surgery and acellular dermal matrix-based surgery.

We will consider requests in the following areas:

  • Non-Invasive Body Contouring
  • Non-Surgical Facial Aesthetics
  • Plastics and Regenerative Medicine

Eye Care

New treatment modalities are a continuing process in improving vision care. We will support educational initiatives on the latest evidence and evolving landscape for eye care treatments.

We provide educational support in the following areas:

  • Increasing understanding of the importance of early treatment and continuum of care with interventional Glaucoma (IG) mindset along the patient treatment journey.
  • Educating healthcare providers on the current unmet needs and the burden of retinal diseases.
  • Increase understanding of dry eye disease as a chronic multifactorial condition of the ocular surface.

We will consider requests in the following areas:

  • Corneal Health (dry eye disease)
  • Glaucoma (angle closure glaucoma, open angle glaucoma)
  • Retina (wet age-related macular degeneration(wet AMD), diabetic macular edema, diabetic retinopathy, including gene therapy)

Other Specialty Areas

We are committed to improving the quality of life for patients with a focus on disease areas where significant unmet need still exists. For example, Exocrine Pancreatic Insufficiency/Pancreatitis.

Ready to apply?

IMPORTANT: Effective January 1, 2024, AbbVie has selected providers to be part of the AbbVie Independent Medical Education (IME) Provider Network.

AbbVie will only consider grant requests submitted by providers who are part of the AbbVie IME Provider Network. Submissions from providers who are not part of the AbbVie IME Provider Network will no longer be considered for support.

Please note: The AbbVie IME Provider Network does not apply to Independent Patient Education, Fellowships, or Call for Grants.

Please contact the Independent Education Team at abbviegrants@abbvie.com if you require assistance.
