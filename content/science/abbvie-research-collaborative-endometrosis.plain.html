Frequently Asked Questions

#### Why am I being asked to participate in this program?

By participating and sharing your health data, our scientists get a unique lens into the different characteristics of your condition, including symptoms, triggers and sources of relief.

#### What are the benefits, risks, and/or consequences of participating in this program?

The AbbVie Research Collaborative is focused on data collection. Your health data is diligently protected from a security standpoint and will only be used for research purposes.

#### Is disclosing my health data required to participate in the Collaborative?

Sharing your medical records is completely optional. If you choose to share your data, it gives our scientists a lens into your health and treatment journey to better understand this disease and hopefully unlock solutions for the future. It's important that you feel comfortable sharing your health information and understand that there are ways to participate without doing so.
