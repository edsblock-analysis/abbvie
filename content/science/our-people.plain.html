What's it like to work for AbbVie R&D?

See our community. Working in AbbVie R&D means being part of a global community where science is sleeves-rolled-up and purpose-driven. Our culture encourages bold ideas, collaboration across disciplines and the persistence it takes to turn discovery into impact for patients.

Who is on the R&D leadership team?

Meet our R&D leaders. AbbVie's R&D leaders combine deep scientific and medical expertise with vision and resolve. They set the course for our pipeline and guide teams that develop new medicines for patients worldwide.

What research publications have AbbVie scientists contributed to?

Explore AbbVie’s research publications. Our science is recognized across the global research community through peer-reviewed publications, collaborations and presentations at leading scientific forums.

How many scientists work at AbbVie?

Thousands of AbbVie scientists worldwide contribute their expertise across the discovery, development, and delivery of medicines. Together, they form a diverse and dynamic team across R&D and operations.

Where are AbbVie's R&D locations?

See our global R&D locations. AbbVie's R&D network spans the globe, with major sites in the United States, Europe and Japan. These locations create an environment where collaboration thrives and innovation accelerates.
