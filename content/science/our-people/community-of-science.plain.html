Advancing research

AbbVie’s Community of Science (ACOS)

We offer initiatives that spark collaboration and accelerate innovation across our global scientific hubs. From speaker series and poster sessions to award programs and networking, ACOS creates opportunities for scientists to engage with each other, test bold ideas and celebrate the power of science.

Inspiring Science

Celebration of Science

This annual event gathers AbbVie scientists from around the world to inspire, discuss and celebrate bold ideas. Our quest to keep exploring and expanding possibilities comes to life at this multi-site event with scientific poster presentations, keynote lectures, patient engagements and networking opportunities.

Honoring Excellence

ACOS Volwiler Honorees

Established in 1985 and named after Dr. Ernest Volwiler, the annual Volwiler Gala celebrates AbbVie’s most distinguished scientists whose research pushes boundaries and drives scientific excellence.

Sparking Breakthroughs

Pathway to Cures

The Pathway to Cures program fuels innovation through two key initiatives. In our annual Innovation Challenge, hundreds of AbbVie scientists pitch bold ideas to senior R&D leaders that tackle some of the most challenging diseases. Colleagues from across functions also collaborate in regular hackathons to solve critical questions and advance concepts toward clinical development.

Next-Gen Scientists

AbbVie Postdoctoral Program

Designed to balance structured learning with cutting-edge research, AbbVie’s Postdoctoral Program provides world-class Ph.D.'s with a premier research environment. The program trains the next generation of scientists while advancing novel, publishable research.

Events at local schools are designed to inspire young scientists and attract top talent.

Our Science University archive contains over 100+ lectures on a diverse range of scientific topics.

Our internal journal allows scientists to share research findings, methodologies and conclusions so we can build upon existing knowledge and explore new frontiers.
