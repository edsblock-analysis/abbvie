How long are internship programs at AbbVie?

Our internships typically run for ten to twelve weeks over the summer, from May to August, and are full-time, paid experiences.

What business areas can interns explore at AbbVie?

We have internship opportunities available in:

  • Commercial
  • Corporate
  • Operations
  • Research & Development

What is the internship experience like at AbbVie?

  • Real, impactful project work
  • Exposure to leaders throughout the organization
  • Skill-building sessions
  • Social events to build your professional network
  • Immersed in a purpose-driven, inclusive culture that fosters curiosity, collaboration, and connection
  • Access to wellness tools and philanthropic initiatives
  • Opportunity to present projects to functional peers and leaders

Where are AbbVie internships located?

We offer internships across ten+ U.S. sites, including:
Irvine, CA; Pleasanton, CA; South San Francisco, CA; Lake County, IL; Cambridge, MA; Waltham, MA; Worcester, MA; Wyandotte, MI; Branchburg, NJ; Florham Park, NJ; Madison, NJ; Waco, TX; Sales field-based locations.

Specific locations may vary depending on your role and function, so please check the individual listing on our career site for more details.

Who is eligible to apply for an AbbVie internship?

If you’re a currently enrolled third-year undergraduate college student, or a graduate college student pursuing a master’s, MBA, PhD, or PharmD degree, then you should consider applying!

Eligibility may vary by program, so refer to individual listings on our career site for specific degree requirements.

Is relocation support available for AbbVie interns?

If eligible, you’ll receive relocation support to help with travel and housing expenses through our relocation vendor.

What is the AbbVie interview process like?

You may go through phone or video interviews, with a mix of behavioral, technical, or case-style questions based on the role.

What are the internship start and end dates?

Internships typically begin in May and end in August, running for approximately 10-12 weeks.

What areas of the business offer internships?

Internships are available across a range of functions, including:

  • Commercial
  • Corporate
  • Operations
  • Research & Development

Is the AbbVie internship paid?

Yes, all internships are paid, full-time positions.
