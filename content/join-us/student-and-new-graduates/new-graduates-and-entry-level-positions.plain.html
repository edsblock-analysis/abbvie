Clinical Sciences Development Program

Launch a dynamic career in clinical sciences through AbbVie’s two-year rotational program designed to build your leadership skills and provide meaningful, hands-on experience. Over the course of three eight-month rotations, you’ll gain the expertise and insights needed to help advance innovative solutions for patients worldwide.

Program highlights

  • Three eight-month rotations across core clinical sciences areas, including:

    • Strategic Operations: Project management, process improvement, knowledge management, and technology
    • Global Patient Safety (Medical Device)
    • R&D Quality Assurance: Aesthetics, medical device quality, audits/inspections, and training

  • Hands-on involvement in projects that directly impact patient care and AbbVie’s global operations

  • Opportunities to develop leadership skills and broaden your professional network through mentorship and collaboration

What you’ll need

  • Bachelor’s or advanced degree in one of the following or related fields: Biology, Business/Marketing, Computer Science, Communications, Data Science/Analytics, Engineering (Biomedical, Bioengineering, Computer, Industrial, or Mechanical), Life Sciences / Medical Sciences
  • Graduation prior to the program start date
  • Strong analytical, problem-solving, and communication skills
  • Passion for advancing patient care through innovation and collaboration

Location Location is primarily Lake County, IL, with the potential for assignments on the West Coast, depending on business needs.

Former interns are strongly considered for our development programs and entry-level roles.

Commercial Leader Program (Aesthetics Track)

Step into the world of aesthetics with real business impact. Our three-year program offers twelve-month rotations where you’ll gain meaningful marketing experience across digital, customer, and field-facing roles, all while building toward a leadership future in one of AbbVie’s most exciting portfolios.

Program highlights

  • Rotational roles in Marketing (Customer, Consumer, Digital), Sales (Strategic Accounts), and Allergan Medical Institute (AMI)
  • Exposure to brand, customer engagement, and training strategy
  • Mentorship and visibility across the aesthetic commercial organization

What you’ll need

  • Enrollment in a full-time MBA program, graduating prior to the program starting
  • At least three years of prior professional experience
  • Strong leadership, communication, and analytical skills
  • Interest in aesthetics, beauty, or luxury brand industries
  • Willingness to relocate to various locations

Location Locations include Irvine, CA

Can I apply? If you're a full-time MBA candidate with a passion for leadership in the aesthetic space, we’d love to hear from you. Former interns are strongly considered for our development programs and entry-level roles.

Commercial Leader Program (Therapeutics Track)

Advance your commercial and leadership career in healthcare through our full-time MBA rotational program. You’ll rotate through three twelve-month assignments in core commercial areas such as Brand Marketing, Field Sales, Market Access, and building broad experience across therapeutic areas.

Program highlights

  • Rotational roles in Brand Marketing, Field Sales, and Market Access
  • Hands-on assignments across our various therapeutic areas
  • Mentorship from industry leaders and team members

What you’ll need

  • Enrollment in a full-time MBA program, graduating prior to the program starting
  • At least three years of prior professional experience
  • Strong leadership, communication, and analytical skills
  • Willingness to relocate to various locations

Location Locations include Irvine, CA; Lake County, IL; Florham Park, NJ, and various U.S. field locations

Can I apply? Absolutely. Our programs are designed to grow your career from day one. You’ll sharpen your strategic thinking, build leadership skills, and gain a deep understanding of the work we do — all while preparing for high-impact roles after the program. Former interns are strongly considered for our development programs and entry-level roles.

Finance Development Program

Build a strong foundation for a leadership career in finance through immersive, hands-on experience across various finance and accounting business functions. Our two-year program combines four, six-month rotational assignments with mentorship, training, and opportunities to network across AbbVie’s global finance operations.

Program highlights

  • Cross-functional assignments in areas such as Commercial, Operations, R&D, and Corporate
  • Development in key disciplines, including financial planning and analysis, treasury, and audit
  • Mentorship, training sessions, and global networking opportunities

What you’ll need

  • Bachelor's or master’s degree in finance or accounting, graduating prior to the program starting
  • Minimum of three accounting courses (three credit hours each) completed by graduation
  • Strong analytical, leadership, and communication skills
  • Willingness to relocate to various locations, with potential for international rotation

Location U.S. program locations include Lake County, IL; Irvine, CA; South San Francisco, CA; Waco, TX; and Puerto Rico, as well as opportunities to relocate to the EU.

Want to go further? The program is designed to prepare you for long-term success, with a proven track record of FDP alumni who’ve advanced to VP and C-suite roles. Former interns are strongly considered for our development programs and entry-level roles.

Human Resources Leadership Development Program (HRLDP)

Create a career that shapes how we support our people. This three-year rotational program is designed for master’s-level HR professionals ready to lead people-first strategy across a global business. You’ll build broad, hands-on experience across core HR areas, including business HR, talent, and HR centers of excellence while developing into a future HR leader.

Program highlights

  • Rotate across core HR areas such as Talent Acquisition, Business HR, Employee Workplace Relations, Total Rewards, or Talent Management
  • End-to-end exposure to AbbVie’s global people strategies and enterprise-wide initiatives
  • Mentorship, coaching, and leadership development throughout the program

What you’ll need

  • Master’s degree in HR or a related field, graduating prior to program starting
  • Previous HR or business-related internship or work experience
  • Strong communication, analytical, and leadership skills
  • Willingness to relocate to various locations, including potential international assignment in year three

Location Locations include Lake County, IL and other U.S. based locations, with the potential for an international assignment in year three

You’ll graduate from the program ready to step into a manager-level role in HR, with the knowledge and confidence to lead with purpose. Former interns are strongly considered for our development programs and entry-level roles.

Operations Development Program (ODP)

Build a global career in operations through real-world, high-impact rotational experiences. Open to engineering and life sciences graduates, this three-year program prepares you for future leadership by rotating through key areas across AbbVie’s global operations network.

Program highlights

  • Rotations across core sub-functions: Manufacturing & Engineering, Quality, Science & Technology, and Supply Chain
  • Exposure to cross-functional teams, strategic projects, and global operations leadership
  • Personalized development, mentorship, and coaching throughout the program

What you’ll need

  • Bachelor’s (U.S.) or master’s degree (outside of U.S.) in chemistry, analytical chemistry, chemical or mechanical engineering, or related field, graduating prior to program starting
  • Strong analytical, problem-solving, and leadership skills
  • Demonstrated ability to collaborate, adapt, and lead in team-based environments
  • Willingness to relocate to various locations within the home country

Location Assignments are typically based in the program member’s home country. U.S. locations include: Irvine, CA; Lake County, IL; Worcester, MA; Branchburg, NJ; Waco, TX. Outside of U.S. locations include: Germany, Ireland, Puerto Rico, Singapore, and more.

You’ll join a community, explore various paths, and play a part in delivering life-changing innovations to patients worldwide.

Still deciding? Externships and internships offer early access and insights into the program. Former interns are strongly considered for our development programs and entry-level roles.

Physician Development Program

Advance your career as a physician-scientist through our two-year rotational program designed for high-potential Medical Doctor (M.D.) specialists. This program is ideal for graduating fellows and physicians who have completed their M.D. with U.S. residency training. You’ll join as an Associate Medical Director, gaining immersive experience across R&D while building the leadership skills you need to drive innovation in drug development.

Program highlights

  • Rotational assignments across core R&D sub-functions, including: a four-month rotation in Clinical Pharmacology & Pharmacometrics and Pharmacovigilance / Safety, a two-month rotation in Medical Affairs, a two-month rotation in Regulatory Affairs, and two eight-month rotations in Clinical Development (early and late stage)
  • Mentorship and professional development through a multi-level mentorship program and leadership-focused training sessions
  • Opportunities to make a direct impact on advancing AbbVie’s pipeline through meaningful, cross-functional work

What you’ll need

  • M.D. degree with U.S. residency training completed
  • Graduating fellowship or recently completed fellowship
  • Strong leadership, collaboration, and communication skills
  • Passion for transitioning into a pharmaceutical R&D career

Location Lake County, IL

Preferred specialties Preference will be given to physicians with specialties in:

  • Oncology
  • Immunology
  • Neuroscience
  • Obesity
  • Endocrinology

Every step of the way, you’ll build critical experience, relationships, and skills to prepare you for long-term success in R&D. The program positions participants to grow into impactful roles within AbbVie, shaping the future of healthcare through scientific innovation.

Frequently asked questions

Expand All Collapse All

What’s the difference between development programs and entry-level roles?

Development programs include rotations, structured learning, and mentorship, while entry-level roles place you directly into a specific full-time position.

Can I apply to more than one program or role?

Yes, you can apply to multiple roles that align with your skills and interests.

Are AbbVie's student and new graduate programs global?

Some programs, like operations and finance, offer global rotations. Others are U.S.-based, but may lead to global rotations over time. Check out our career site for more specific information on roles within your country of interest.

Does AbbVie offer relocation support for development programs and entry-level roles?

Relocation offerings are dependent on the candidate's role and eligibility.

What is the AbbVie interview process like?

You may go through phone or video interviews, with a mix of behavioral, technical, or case-style questions based on the role.
