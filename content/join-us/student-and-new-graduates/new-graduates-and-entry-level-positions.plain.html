Our broad range of development programs allows recent college graduates to grow their careers through real responsibility, meaningful collaboration and structured learning.

Fellowships

Turn your PharmD or PhD into industry impact.

Designed for PharmD or PhD graduates, our one- to two-year fellowship program offers the chance to grow into an industry profession through advanced development opportunities. With the support of AbbVie’s experienced teams and partner institutions, you’ll build the skills and experience needed to succeed in the pharmaceutical world.

Real-world assignments

Hands-on projects alongside AbbVie professionals

Entry-level opportunities

Start strong with work that matters.

Our entry-level roles give recent graduates the chance to dive straight into impactful work. Whether you’ve come through one of our student programs or not, all you need is a passion for the pharmaceutical industry and the relevant qualifications, which are dependent on the role you are seeking. You’ll contribute to meaningful work, sharpen your expertise, and build a strong foundation for what’s next.
