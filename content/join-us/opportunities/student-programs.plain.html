why abbvie

With a presence in over 70 countries and a mission to positively impact more than 60 million lives each year, we create opportunities for students and new graduates to gain real-world experience, mentorship, and grow through innovation-driven programs.

Our inclusive, collaborative, and innovative culture is grounded in the Ways We Work, which guides how we support one another and make a lasting impact. Frequently asked questions

Expand All Collapse All

When should I apply for internships, co-ops, or development programs?

Most U.S. roles open in early fall (August to October), with some spring openings. Check our career site regularly for the latest opportunities.

Do I need experience to apply for student or new graduate roles?

While experience is not always required for every position, it can be beneficial. We also look for relevant internships, co-ops, volunteer work, or leadership roles in student organizations, as these can demonstrate valuable skills.

Are AbbVie programs available outside the U.S.?

Yes, AbbVie offers global programs and country-specific roles, along with some opportunities for international rotations within our development programs. Search by city/town on our careers site.

What is the AbbVie interview process like?

You may go through phone or video interviews, with a mix of behavioral, technical, or case-style questions based on the role.
