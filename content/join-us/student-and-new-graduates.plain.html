why abbvie

With a presence in over 70 countries and a mission to positively impact more than 60 million lives each year, we create opportunities for students and new graduates to gain real-world experience, mentorship, and grow through innovation-driven programs.

Our inclusive, collaborative, and innovative culture is grounded in the Ways We Work, which guides how we support one another and make a lasting impact. We believe that how we achieve breakthroughs matters just as much as the results, creating a workplace where people and their careers can truly thrive.

With $63 billion invested in research and development since 2013 and partnerships with over 250 institutions, AbbVie offers you the chance to shape a healthier, more hopeful future while building a career with purpose.
