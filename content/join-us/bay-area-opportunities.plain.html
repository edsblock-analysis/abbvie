Pioneering breakthroughs for patients

We discover and develop innovative medicines that help solve serious health issues for people around the world. In South San Francisco, we are driving AbbVie’s leadership in oncology and immunology, and using precision medicine to unlock more targeted treatments for patients. Beyond South San Francisco, we have a presence throughout the Bay Area, which includes sites that became part of AbbVie through our acquisition of Allergan in 2020.
