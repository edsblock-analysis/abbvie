Life at AbbVie

We believe the paths we take to achieve our breakthroughs matter. How we get there is just as vital as the outcome.

Inclusive. Collaborative. Innovative.

Since day one our culture has been the secret to our success. It's reflected in everything we do and everything we offer. At the foundation are the Ways We Work, five pillars that guide how people work, innovate, collaborate and treat one another. It’s what enables individuals, teams and our company to make a real difference in people’s lives and makes AbbVie a great place to work.

All For One AbbVie

We weigh all our decisions against the common good for the enterprise. We inspire, share and create as an inclusive team. We solve problems for all rather than serving our immediate team.

Decide, Smart & Sure

We make intelligent decisions, adapting to create the best outcomes. While never compromising patient health or safety, we act quickly, embrace experimentation, accept what doesn’t work and get on to what does.

Agile & Accountable

We seek to streamline and eliminate unnecessary obstacles. We plan ahead and adapt as we go. We delegate and make tough decisions to ensure focus on priorities and results, staying keenly aware of the urgency in all we do.

Clear & Courageous

Open, honest and candid dialogue is core to our work and how we act with others. We share information freely and continuously because insights and solutions can come from anywhere and ultimately serve us all. We admit mistakes. Even in disagreement, we grow stronger by putting the courage of our convictions to the test.

Make Possibilities Real

We question relentlessly with endless curiosity. We’re never satisfied with “good enough”—patients depend on us to continually deliver more. We challenge ourselves to find creative, constructive solutions to turn possibilities into reality.

AbbVie's culture is dynamic, diverse and different. I think it's a unique place to be because we do so much and what we make impacts people's lives.

Urvaash Sukhu
Manager, Finance, AbbVie
