Women Leaders in Action

We are a powerful catalyst that enables and empowers employees to reach their full potential. Women Leaders in Action (WLA) is open to all employees.

There’s a future full of possibilities

We encourage all qualified candidates to apply for open positions. Join us and make a remarkable impact.

Are you interested? Join Us.

What we stand for

Women represent more than 50% of the world’s population – making this group an essential part of our workforce, communities, patients and customers.

Women Leaders in Action enables all employees to reach their full potential through:

  • Empowering and inspiring employees to take charge of their career journey and achieve their full potential
  • Attracting and building future leaders
  • Delivering business results and value by accessing the unique insights women can provide

Working at AbbVie

Skill development is one of our strategic priorities. We do this through activities such as:

  • Seminars to empower our members to activate their career development.
  • Informal networking and mentorship opportunities, which help improve retention and employee satisfaction.

Making an impact

We are committed to helping employees achieve their full potential and support community organizations where we work and live. We focus on engaging with our local communities, amplifying our impact beyond bringing effective medicines to market. This includes volunteering for events that teach the importance of STEM-based curriculum with school-age children in underserved schools.

WLA is proud to support community organizations in partnership with the AbbVie Foundation.
