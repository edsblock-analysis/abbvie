The time is AHORA

We bring together Latinx/Hispanic employees and their allies to contribute to the success of AbbVie. AHORA is open to all AbbVie employees.

There’s a future full of possibilities

We encourage all qualified candidates to apply for open positions. Join us and make a remarkable impact.

What we stand for

AHORA recognizes, celebrates and educates others about Latinx/Hispanic culture.

AHORA focuses on:

  • Informal mentorship and networking
  • Supporting business initiatives
  • Cultural awareness and visibility
  • Community Assistance

Working at AbbVie

True to our vision of helping employees reach their full potential, AHORA emphasizes mentorship and networking opportunities.

Our efforts include:

  • Hosting “Café con Leche” talks with AbbVie leadership
  • Offering seminars led by university partners
  • Collaborating with external organizations
  • Supporting employees new to AbbVie with orientation activities

Making an impact

Community outreach is part of AHORA’s fabric. True to our vision of building a stronger workforce, we are committed to supporting the communities in our own backyard.

  • We’ve embedded with a high school in our hometown community of Waukegan, IL and support their students with mentoring along with career exploration, including encouraging their students to apply to our internships
  • We engage in various volunteer efforts, including mentorship for students from diverse backgrounds
  • We partner with other organizations to find ways to collaborate and benchmark on Latino ERG initiatives and activities and provide networking and mentorship workshops

AHORA is proud to support community organizations in partnership with the AbbVie Foundation.
