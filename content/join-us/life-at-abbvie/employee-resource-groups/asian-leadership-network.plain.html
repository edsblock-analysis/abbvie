Asian Leadership Network

AbbVie’s Asian Leadership Network (ALN) drives inclusion and increases awareness of cultural values, heritages and traditions in Asian communities. ALN is open to all AbbVie employees.

What we stand for

With 48 countries and nearly 2,300 languages, Asia is defined by a wide spectrum of customs, religious beliefs and cultures. The Asian Leadership Network fosters belonging and inclusion and fosters a workplace where every voice is heard and valued.

The Asian Leadership Network fosters a diverse and inclusive environment where every voice is heard and valued. ALN promotes a sense of belonging and community by enabling all employees to reach their full potential. We do this through:

  • Raising awareness about cultural diversity
  • Informal mentoring and networking
  • Business and patient impact

Working at AbbVie

Perspective matters. Our patient population is diverse, and their needs are complex. We solve these challenges together, because complex problems require innovative solutions built on the diverse perspectives of an entire team.

We are committed to having our members achieve their full potential. Our efforts include:

  • Cultural inclusion and peer mentoring programs to create opportunities for our members to excel in leadership skills, expand their network and inspire cross-functional collaborations
  • “Lunch and Learn” programs to enable our members to better understand our products and pipeline

Making an impact

ALN is proud to support community organizations in partnership with the AbbVie Foundation.
