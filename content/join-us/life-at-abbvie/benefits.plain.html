Benefits

Support at every stage in your career and life.

Our compensation, benefits, and well-being programs support your diverse and evolving needs at every stage of your career and life. We offer differentiated and competitive rewards and benefits. Some programs vary by country.

What we offer

Expand All Collapse All

Compensation

  • Competitive base pay
  • Short- and long-term incentives
  • Recognition programs

Benefits

  • Comprehensive medical, dental, vision, mental health and prescription drug coverage
  • Sick pay and medical leave
  • Life and disability insurance
  • Flexible spending accounts to help pay for health expenses using pre-tax dollars*
  • Retirement Savings Plan
  • Employee Stock Purchase Plan, available in most countries outside the U.S.
  • Housing and transport allowances, available outside the U.S.

* Available in the U.S.

Flexible work and time off

  • Workplace flexibility that varies based on role, such as flex time, part-time work, job sharing, shift changes, compressed work weeks, and remote work
  • Hybrid work model for office-based employees that consists of three minimum set days in the office and two days of remote work per week
  • A phased retirement program*
  • Paid vacation and company holidays
  • Two paid days off to volunteer, in addition to company-wide volunteer week

*Available in the U.S.

Support for families

  • Paid leave for new parents welcoming a child through birth, adoption, surrogacy or fostering
  • Paid leave and support for people caring for sick family members
  • Support for family planning, including adoption and surrogacy assistance*
  • Discounts at U.S childcare centers, plus access to an on-site daycare center at our headquarters in Lake County, IL

*Available in the U.S.

Holistic well-being

We partner with employees on their well-being journey, connecting them to tools, and support to help them be their best selves, at work and at home. Because when you're at your best, you can make the most impact.

Learning & development

We're at our best when we push ourselves to grow, try new skills and have the courage to take risks. Our commitment to learning and development provides resources that empower employees to step up and reach higher.
