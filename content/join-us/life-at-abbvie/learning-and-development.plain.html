Reach higher ground

We’re at our best when we push ourselves to grow, try new skills and have the courage to take risks. Aligning your interests with our imperatives as a business, you always have opportunities to step up and reach higher. We don’t just hope everyone at AbbVie will push themselves further, we expect it and support it through a combination of personalized learning opportunities and companywide development programs.

Your development, your way

We believe in employees owning their growth and development. Employees define their goals, and AbbVie provides resources to help achieve them. From workshops to global events, our offerings ensure your growth journey is easily accessible, personalized and tailored to your interests.

For example, during our award-winning annual Learn.Develop.Perform (LDP) Week, global leaders and experts share their insights on topics like collaboration, business acumen and unconscious bias. Employees curate their learning experience, choosing topics that resonate most with their goals.

We constantly innovate how learning is delivered. Past events have featured a 24-hour radio station, bite-sized video content through a YouTube channel and TED-style presentations.

Develop as a leader

Great leaders bring out the best in their teams. We’re committed to providing leaders with the tools to succeed in an ever-evolving landscape. Whether you’re a new, aspiring or experienced leader, AbbVie offers programs to help you grow and inspire others.

Programs include:

  • Development Workshops, solutions and resources that enable leaders to advance their skills, expand their knowledge, amplify our culture, and progress in their careers
  • Leading at AbbVie, a cohort-based program that supports first-time people leaders with critical skills needed to successfully lead individuals and teams
  • Key Talent Leadership Programs, a suite of customized learning solutions designed for key talent leaders selected by their business for accelerated development
  • Executive Mentoring Program (EMP), pairs high performing talent with AbbVie senior executive leaders for a one-on-one mentoring partnership
  • Executive Sponsorship Program (ESP), two-year program focused on building enterprise leadership capabilities, creating development opportunities, and providing cross-functional visibility for key talent leaders

Learning anytime, anywhere

No matter your role, level or background, we make sure you have access to the tools and resources needed to grow on your terms. This includes:

  • On-demand webinars delivered by global content experts and AbbVie leaders
  • Talent Link, an internal platform to build your network and grow your brand
  • Tuition reimbursement in the U.S.
