EXCEL Externships

Curious about where your degree could take you?

Our externship opens the door to real project exposure, valuable networking, and professional development over two days. Designed for second-year undergraduate college students, this voluntary program is held annually in August and offers a firsthand introduction to the pharmaceutical industry and a head start on your career journey.

Two-day, immersive voluntary experience

Lake County, IL

Hands-on case study competition

Networking and professional development sessions

details

EXCEL Externship Experience

  • Networking with functional leaders, mentors, and peers
  • Exposure to our broad business areas and culture
  • Support from functional business members and recruitment team
  • Interview for an AbbVie internship for the following summer

Location AbbVie Headquarters, Lake County, IL

Can I apply? If you’re a second-year undergraduate college student with an interest in exploring future opportunities in the pharmaceutical industry, then yes!

AbbVie provides travel and accommodation support for eligible students and offers the potential to interview for the following summer’s internship program.

With us, you’ll have the full support of the current program members, the functional team, and the recruitment team. We can’t wait to hear from you!

What it’s like

Get real-world experience, grow your skills, and make an impact during our full-time, paid internship.

Held every year from May to August, our ten to twelve-week program allows students to work on meaningful projects while exploring future career opportunities.

Ten to twelve-week paid opportunity

Hands-on, cross-functional projects

Networking and mentorship with business professionals

Multiple locations across the U.S.

details

Internship Experience

  • Gain impactful industry experience in various functional areas
  • Exposure to senior leaders
  • Build valuable skills and grow professionally
  • Team-based socials and networking
  • Opportunity to be considered for our full-time roles

Location Opportunities are available in ten+ locations across the U.S., including:
Irvine, CA; South San Francisco, CA; Lake County, IL; Cambridge, MA; Worcester, MA; Branchburg, NJ; Waco, TX, and more!

Can I apply? Yes, if you’re a currently enrolled undergraduate or graduate student pursuing a degree in a relevant field. Roles vary by function, so check job descriptions and required qualifications for details.

Still deciding? Our internships offer relocation support for eligible students. You’ll also receive:

  • Competitive pay
  • Paid company holidays
  • Retirement savings plan
  • Well-being tools, resources, and support

Former interns are strongly considered for our development program and entry-level roles.

Co-ops

Step beyond the classroom into a role where you’ll lead with purpose and gain robust experience.

Over four to six months, our paid co-op program gives currently enrolled undergraduate and graduate college students the chance to contribute to critical projects across multiple business areas while being supported by a functional team that’s invested in your growth.

Four- to six-month time period in Operations and in Business Technology Solutions

Hands-on projects alongside AbbVie professionals

Various benefits and perks

Apply your school knowledge to real-world work

details

Co-op Experience

  • Build a professional network
  • Mentorship and learning opportunities
  • Gain robust work experience for an extended duration of time
  • Contribute to projects across multiple business functions

Location Lake County, IL or Worcester, MA.

Can I apply? If you're currently an enrolled undergraduate or graduate college student pursuing a degree in a relevant field, and looking for expansive, functional experience in a collaborative setting, we’d love to hear from you.

If you’re still thinking about it, AbbVie provides relocation and travel support for eligible students. Plus, you’ll also get:

  • Competitive pay
  • Paid company holidays
  • Retirement savings plan
  • Well-being tools, resources, and support

If you like what you hear, our co-op program could be your next step!

Frequently asked questions

Expand All Collapse All

When do AbbVie applications open?

Most roles open in early fall (August to October). Visit our careers site regularly for updates on internships, co-ops, and externships.

Where are AbbVie's Student and New Graduate roles located?

We offer roles in 10+ U.S. locations, including Lake County, IL; Irvine, CA; Worcester, MA; and others. Location varies by role.

Does AbbVie offer relocation support for interns or co-ops?

Relocation offerings are dependent on the candidate's role and eligibility.

What is the AbbVie interview process like?

You may go through phone or video interviews, with a mix of behavioral, technical, or case-style questions based on the role.
