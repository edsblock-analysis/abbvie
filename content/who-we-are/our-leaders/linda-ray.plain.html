Linda Ray

Senior Vice President, Business Technology Solutions

Linda Ray is Senior Vice President, Business Technology Solutions, responsible for AbbVie’s technology systems and data assets, focused on enabling business growth and operational effectiveness priorities.

Ms. Ray has more than 30 years of diverse experience. She joined AbbVie in 2017 and most recently served as Vice President, Business Technology Solutions, Operations and Enterprise Technology. Prior to joining AbbVie, Ms. Ray held technology roles of increasing responsibility at W.W. Grainger, and also has a background in human resources.

Ms. Ray holds a bachelor’s degree in communication and a master’s degree in organizational communication from the University of Colorado. She also holds a master’s degree in business administration from William E. Simon Graduate School of Business Administration, University of Rochester – New York.
