Striking Out Cancer by Turning Ks into Ws

For every strikeout thrown by Cubs pitchers at Wrigley Field this season, AbbVie is donating to charities that help fight cancer and support cancer patients and their families.

Donations to
fight cancer
with every
Cubs strikeout.

In 2026, AbbVie will donate $241 for every strikeout thrown by a Cubs pitcher at Wrigley Field this season, honoring the roughly 241 Americans diagnosed with cancer every hour.1

AbbVie has pledged up to $250,000 per season in donations to support this cause.

1. http://cancerstatisticscenter.cancer.org

As a cancer survivor myself, I am proud to be part of the AbbVie Striking Out Cancer program. Each time I step onto the mound, it means even more knowing that AbbVie is supporting the fight against cancer with every strikeout I throw.

Jameson Taillon
Chicago Cubs Pitcher

Striking Out Cancer donations to benefit not-for-profit organizations working to advance the fight against cancer, including Conquer Cancer, the ASCO Foundation, a 501(c)(3) charitable organization focused on accelerating breakthroughs in lifesaving research and empowering people everywhere to conquer cancer.

Pictured (left to right): Cubs pitcher Jameson Taillon, AbbVie CFO Scott Reents and VP and Executive Director of Cubs Charities, Lea Jesse.

At the conclusion of the 2025 Cubs season, AbbVie donated $250,000 to Cubs Charities. The amount marked the largest donation to Cubs Charities to date in support of the fight against cancer.
