Impact through Inclusion

We are strongly committed to valuing diverse perspectives, creating a culture that embraces inclusion and belonging and treating all with dignity and respect.

Inclusion is for all people, at all places and at all times

Inclusion unlocks performance and growth, so we can continue to make possibilities real for our patients and customers around the world.

Our actions speak for themselves

We are constantly striving to raise the bar in our workplace and in the communities where we work and live.

Building a world-class workforce

  • Through inclusive education programs, we give colleagues around the world the opportunity to learn from one another while we build high performing teams.
  • We prioritize leadership development and provide one-on-one mentoring that pairs high performing talent with AbbVie senior executives.


Driving an inclusive culture through awareness and understanding

  • We believe that having a voice is about more than volume – it is about belonging – feeling empowered to share your unique perspective as part of a collaborative, high-performing team.
  • Our employee resource groups are open to all employees and reflect our belief that inclusion benefits everyone and drives high performance.
  • We are committed to accessibility for all – so our employees can reach their full potential.

Creating connections and fostering well-being

  • We partner with employees on their well-being journey, connecting them to tools and support at work and at home.
  • We’ve made dialogue an everyday way of life at AbbVie, fostering connection to one another and creating space for all to contribute their unique value.
  • As part of AbbVie’s commitment to building community, our Employee Resource Groups (ERGs) partner with the AbbVie Foundation to support a variety of nonprofit organizations.
