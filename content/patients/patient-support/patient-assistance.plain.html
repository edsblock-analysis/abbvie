AbbVie’s Patient Assistance Overview

AbbVie is committed to helping patients get the medicines they need. That’s why we offer patient assistance programs that provide free AbbVie medicines to qualifying patients. Our Patient Assistance Programs are intended for people that live in the United States, have limited or no health insurance coverage and demonstrate qualifying financial need. In 2024, we assisted more than 235,000 people.

Applying to myAbbVie Assist is simple. It is free to apply, and those who qualify will receive their medicine for free: no co-pays or shipping costs.
