Decade of Impact

Celebrating the close of our historic, five-year $350 million grants to nine nonprofit partners.

Making a real difference for patients, communities and society

2023 marked the close of our historic five-year, $350 million commitment to create long-term impact through donations to nine non-profit organizations providing equitable access to health care, addressing systemic barriers in education and building community resiliency. This commitment has touched hundreds of thousands of lives and exemplifies the responsibility and privilege of everyone at AbbVie to support our communities, better our society and serve patients.
