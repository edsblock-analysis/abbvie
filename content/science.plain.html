Frequently asked questions

Expand All Collapse All

What diseases and conditions does AbbVie develop medicines for?

AbbVie’s research spans areas where we can make the biggest impact: immunology, oncology, neuroscience, eye care and medical aesthetics. Our scientists are tackling complex diseases in each field, dedicated to advancing therapies that can transform lives. Learn more about our areas of focus.

What kinds of new technology does AbbVie use in its research?

From using AI to optimize drug design, to wearable devices that capture patient data in real time, to advanced robotics that streamline manufacturing, AbbVie leverages technology to enhance speed, quality and precision in R&D. These innovations expand the capabilities of our teams, empowering them to push boundaries further. See how we’re shaping the future of innovation.

How is AbbVie leveraging artificial intelligence (AI)?

AI and machine learning are helping us accelerate every stage of drug discovery, from predicting how molecules will behave, to designing therapies faster, to reducing trial-and-error in the lab. These tools allow our scientists to see patterns that were once invisible, speeding up the path to new medicines. Explore AI and data convergence at AbbVie.

How can I learn more about the R&D community at AbbVie?

Learn more about the people and teams shaping the future of science at AbbVie. Our scientists aren’t just advancing medicine, they are growing careers, sharing knowledge and working together in a culture built on curiosity and collaboration.
