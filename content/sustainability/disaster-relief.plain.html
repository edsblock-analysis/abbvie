Disaster & Humanitarian Relief

Our commitment to creating a positive impact on the world is at the heart of everything we do. We aid communities across the globe affected by natural disasters and humanitarian crises by donating essential AbbVie medicines, equipping nonprofit partners with needed resources and providing financial support to impacted employees and their families.

The AbbVie Foundation also matches employee donations to select organizations supporting relief efforts in affected areas, empowering our employees to make a difference and advance a healthier world in times of critical need.

Supporting our communities

Southern California Wildfires The AbbVie Foundation donated $1 million to support our disaster relief partners providing safe shelter, food, essential supplies, and critical health and other services to residents and communities impacted by the wildfires across Southern California.

Hurricanes Helene and Milton The AbbVie Foundation supported relief and recovery efforts through a $250,000 donation to our trusted nonprofit partners. We also donated essential medicines to ensure people impacted by the hurricanes could maintain their health during this challenging time.

Israel and Gaza We donated $1 million to trusted humanitarian relief organizations in response to the conflict in Israel and Gaza. The AbbVie Foundation also matched employee donations to select humanitarian relief partners and nonprofits standing against hate, violence, injustice and persecution of any kind.

Ukraine We partnered with nonprofit organizations to support relief efforts in Ukraine and surrounding countries, and ensure patients continue to have access to our life-saving medicines.

Turkey & Syria
We donated $1 million to disaster response and relief efforts in the wake of the devastating earthquakes in Turkey and Syria.

Puerto Rico
Following two major hurricanes in 2018, we made a five-year, $100 million donation to support immediate recovery efforts and long-term resiliency on the island.
