Environmental, Social & Governance

We’re advancing ESG initiatives that contribute to our sustainable growth, so that we can create a positive impact for generations to come.

Our leaders are stewards of our environmental, social and governance (ESG) mission, and our people are committed to ensuring our research, innovations and collaborations put impact for people, communities and our world first.

OUR APPROACH

Our ESG framework is built around three foundational pillars

We see our framework as an opportunity to apply the same rigor, curiosity and focus on science-based solutions to how we sustainably maintain and grow our business.

We discover and deliver innovative medicines that solve serious health issues.

We unlock the full potential of our global teams and partners to deliver today and into the future.

We innovate with integrity and intention to advance long-term patient health and business resiliency.

Our material drivers

We periodically conduct materiality assessments to inform which environmental, social & governance (ESG) issues are most relevant to our business and our stakeholders. Reviewed and approved by executive leadership, we identified our material ESG topics by gathering and analyzing insights and feedback from a variety of internal and external stakeholders, including senior cross-functional ​leadership, employees, investors, community partners, patients, regulators and more.
