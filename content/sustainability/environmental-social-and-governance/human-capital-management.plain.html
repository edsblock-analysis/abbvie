Human Capital Management

We develop talent to solve complex health challenges and make a real difference in people’s lives.

The wide range of knowledge, perspectives and capabilities of our people is critical to AbbVie’s success in both the short and long term. To unlock their full potential, we focus on attracting and retaining the best talent, embracing equity, equality, diversity and inclusion (EEDI), advancing professional and leadership development and enhancing employee well-being.

Enhancing well-being

We support work-life well-being and flexibility to help our people sustain high performance, productivity and engagement.

Some examples of the support we provide include parental and caregiver leave, flexibility, mental health resources and emergency relief. Through our Employee Relief Program (EAP), we also assist eligible employees during emergencies. In 2022, access to the EAP was expanded to all countries.
