AbbVie believes in transparency–from our clinical trial processes and results to our partnerships and how we engage in public policymaking.

As part of our uncompromising commitment to transparency, we measure the progress of our environmental, social and governance (ESG) efforts relative to various external frameworks, including the:

  • S&P Global Corporate Sustainability Assessment

  • Task Force on Climate-Related Financial Disclosures

  • United Nations Sustainability Development Goals

  • Sustainability Accounting Standards Board

  • Carbon Disclosure Project (CDP)
